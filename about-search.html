About Ymal Search

Our Mission

Ymal Search was created to provide a private alternative to mainstream search engines. We believe that search should be fast, accurate, and respect your privacy.

How It Works

  1. You search: Type your query on search.ymal.space
  2. We fetch: Your query is sent to multiple search sources anonymously
  3. We clean: Results are stripped of tracking parameters
  4. You get results: Clean, relevant results without tracking

Privacy Features

No Tracking

We don't track your searches or store personal information.

No Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for essential functions.

No Profiling

We don't create profiles or target ads based on your searches.

Search Shortcuts

Use these shortcuts for specific searches:

!yt - Search YouTube !w - Search Wikipedia !r - Search Reddit !img - Search images !news - Search news

Technical Details

Ymal Search aggregates results from multiple privacy-respecting search sources. Our frontend is built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript for maximum speed and privacy. We use serverless functions to handle search requests without storing any user data.
